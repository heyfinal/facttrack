## Curative Detection Rules (the actual product)

MVP = 17 rules (revised per DeepSeek-R1 review). Each rule emits a structured finding with severity, confidence score, dollar impact estimate (configurable price deck), and a recommended action.

1. **Unrecorded RRC P-4 assignment**: RRC operator-of-record change with no corresponding assignment recorded in county OPR within N days
2. **Probate gap**: lessor / mineral owner death indicated by deed-record patterns or external probate databases, but no AOH (Affidavit of Heirship) or probate decree recorded
3. **Stranger to title** (BOOSTED): RRC operator or county-recorded lessee appears in chain without a clear acquisition path; cross-checks RRC P-4 history vs county deed books vs GLO state lease records to close the loop
4. **Depth severance mismatch**: lease grants limited depth interval, RRC production data shows production from outside the granted interval
5. **Lease primary term expiring + no continuous production**: lease nearing end of primary term with no RRC PR data showing continuous production
6. **Pugh-clause acreage release**: continuous-development clause triggers partial acreage release after primary term, often missed by operators
7. **Retained-acreage well miss**: continuous-development well not drilled in time, retained-acreage clause forfeits non-producing acreage
8. **Lease Assignment NRI Mismatch** (PUBLIC-DATA only): NRI implied by chain of recorded assignments / partial releases doesn't reconcile to what's recorded in current operator filings. Tolerance threshold ±0.0005 decimal. (Well-paydeck-level NRI variance is a separate rule that needs operator data — OFF MVP.)
9. **Unratified extension**: lease extension recorded but not signed by all required parties
10. **Missing lease ratification** for unitized tracts
11. **Heirship affidavit older than ten years** without supporting probate
12. **Top-lease conflict** (a lease executed while a prior lease still in primary term)
13. **Surface use disputes** indicated by separate surface owner records vs lease grant
14. **Pipeline ROW expiration** (where county records expose this)
15. **County / state mineral classification mismatch** (state-owned mineral claimed by private operator)
16. **Mineral/Royalty Ambiguity in Conveyance** (NEW per DeepSeek-R1): East-TX heirship conveyances frequently use "1/2 interest" or "1/4 interest" without specifying mineral vs royalty — flag for landman + attorney interpretation. Pattern-detects ambiguous interest-type language in scanned deed text.
17. **ORRI Cloud** (NEW per DeepSeek-R1): unreleased Overriding Royalty Interest from prior operator, recorded but never released after lease termination >36 months ago. Common East-TX acquisition blocker.

**Future rule additions (post-MVP):** Well Paydeck NRI Variance (needs operator decks under NDA), force-pooling tracking, RRC hearing tracking, surface damage release tracking, division-order audit (needs operator decks).
